Meatloaf

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 cup ketchup

Instruction

Step 1: Preheat Oven

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2: Mix Ingredients

In a large bowl, combine the beef, bread, milk, egg, onion, salt and pepper. Mix well and place in a lightly greased 5x9 inch loaf pan, OR form into a loaf and place in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish.

Step 3: Add Ketchup

Pour the ketchup over the top of the loaf.

Step 4: Bake

Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
